The Fool

Arcana: Major Arcana

Quick Summary: New beginnings—a leap of faith with no guarantees.

Keywords

  • new beginnings
  • taking risks
  • innocence
  • naivete
  • foolishness
  • recklessness

Upright Interpretation

You're at a threshold: starting fresh, trusting the unknown, or taking a leap. This card asks: are you ready to not know the answer before you jump?

Reversed Interpretation

Fear is holding you back, or you're being genuinely reckless. The difference: intuition + preparation vs. impulse + no plan. Check your grounding.

Tip

Ask: What would I do if I trusted myself? Pick one small leap—not a cliff dive.
