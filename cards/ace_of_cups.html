Minor Arcana · Cups

Ace of Cups

Emotional renewal, heartfelt openness, and the first spark of intimacy.

Keywords

Love · Compassion · Creativity · Renewal · Spiritual connection

Upright

A tender beginning in relationships or creative work. Let yourself feel deeply and receive support.

Reversed

Emotional overwhelm or blocked feelings. Slow down and create space for gentle honesty.

Reflection prompt

Where can you soften this week? Identify one place to accept help or open your heart.
