Major Arcana · 18

The Moon

Intuition, dreams, and the unseen currents below the surface.

Keywords

Intuition · Mystery · Reflection · Emotions · Subconscious

Upright

Trust your inner voice and embrace the unknown. The Moon asks you to sit with uncertainty and listen to what arises.

Reversed

Confusion lifts when you ground yourself in reality. Seek clarity before acting on assumptions.

Reflection prompt

Where are you being guided by intuition, and where do you need more facts? Name one place to seek clarity.
